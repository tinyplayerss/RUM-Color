How to Use RUM-Color

  1. Welcome to RUM-Color Click on any color Circle to copy its hex value to your clipboard. The hex value will be displayed for you.

  2. Explore the Rumble Color Palette and the Rumble Studio Color Palette below. Colors are conveniently organized for your design and development projects.

  3. Check the "Clipboard History" section to see a log of copied colors.

  4. Enjoy using RUM-Color Palette for your creative endeavors!
